## 📋 Executive Summary

India is home to **780+ languages**, yet **197+ are endangered** and at risk of disappearing forever. This project is a comprehensive initiative to document, preserve, and revitalize India's endangered linguistic heritage using modern technology, community participation, and AI.

## 🎯 Mission Statement

Every 14 days, a language dies. When a language disappears, an entire cultural universe vanishes with it—stories, songs, medicinal knowledge, historical memory, and unique ways of understanding the world.

Our mission is to:
- **Document** endangered languages before they disappear
- **Preserve** audio recordings, texts, and cultural context
- **Revitalize** through education and technology
- **Connect** speakers with resources and each other

---

## 💼 Business Context & Impact

### The Problem
- **197+ Indian languages** are classified as endangered by UNESCO
- **42 languages** have fewer than 10,000 speakers
- **5 languages** have only 1 surviving speaker
- Many languages lack written scripts, making documentation critical

### The Opportunity
- **Cultural Heritage:** Preserve 5,000+ years of linguistic diversity
- **Educational Value:** Language learning platforms, research materials
- **Technology Integration:** Speech recognition, translation tools for underserved languages
- **Community Impact:** Empower minority communities, preserve traditional knowledge

### Target Beneficiaries
1. **Indigenous Communities** - Preserve identity and heritage
2. **Researchers & Linguists** - Access to rare language data
3. **Educational Institutions** - Curriculum materials
4. **Technology Companies** - Training data for low-resource languages
5. **Government & NGOs** - Policy-making and preservation programs
